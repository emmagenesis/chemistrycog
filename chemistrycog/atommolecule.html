Know Your Atoms from Your Molecules

It's essential to know what an atom is vs what a molecule is. A molecule is made up of two or more atoms chemically joined together.

Examples of things only made of atoms:

  • Argon
  • Helium
  • Neon
  • All the nobles gases!

Examples of things made of molecules:

  • Water
  • Oxygen
  • Carbon dioxide
  • Nitrogen

Examples of things NOT made of molecules:

  • Sodium chloride
  • Iron
  • aluminium oxide
  • Diamond
