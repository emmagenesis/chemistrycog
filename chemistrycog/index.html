About the author

The development of this website came about as a way of broadening support for chemistry students while sharing the 18 years of experience I have had teaching in a range of secondary schools in England. After playing around with Python a few years ago, the opportunity to challenge myself to write code to build my own website was an opportunity that could not be missed.

The website structure and layout started with the application of html and CSS to give the overall structure and look. Future projects will include using Javascript to give more interactivity and functionality for users. For those local to Harrogate, I offer tutoring sessions in person.

About Learning Chemistry

This section is aimed toward students studying chemistry at GCSE or A Level (or, in fact, anyone between the ages of 13-18 studying chemistry!). The focus will on content from Edexcel IGCSE Chemistry and AQA A Level Chemistry, but can easily be applied to many of the ideas in other syllabuses from UK exam boards.

About Teaching Chemistry

This section of the site contains key areas of content where students find they become "stuck" or can develop misconceptions and difficulties in getting their head around tricky concepts. You will find articles and resources developed to support students in picking apart problems and give them the tools to tackle them with more confidence.
